NewsBlur 14 is a major update with new features, a redesigned interface, and full support for iOS/iPadOS 26 and macOS Tahoe.

New features:
- Dashboard: A new Dashboard at the top of the feeds list shows stories from your favorite feeds, updated every five minutes. Add, remove, rearrange, and select feeds to watch news arrive throughout the day.
- Ask AI: Ask questions about any story using your choice of AI model. Includes a thinking mode toggle for deeper analysis.
- Pinch-to-zoom images: Pinch any image in a story to open a full-sized Quick Look preview.
- List and Magazine views: Two new story layout options alongside the existing Grid view. List shows compact rows; Magazine shows taller rows with larger thumbnails.
- Mark Story Read options: New preferences to mark stories read on scroll, on selection, after an interval, or manually. A Mark All Read button appears at the end of the story list when not marking on scroll.
- Premium Pro tier: A new subscription tier with higher limits and additional features, now shown in the iOS upgrade dialog.
- Custom feed and folder icons: Feeds and folders can now display custom icons.
- Collapse-all / expand-all button added to All Site Stories.
- Unmute support for individual feeds.

iOS and iPadOS 26 / macOS Tahoe support:
- Modernized menu bar on Mac and iPad with full keyboard shortcut support.
- Simplified column layout options: feeds beside or over the story detail.
- Draggable column divider between feeds and stories on iPad, with a pointer cursor on hover.
- Auto-collapse sidebar when the story column gets too narrow; uses iPhone layout when very narrow.
- Sidebar toggle buttons for showing and hiding the feeds list.
- Transparent toolbar styling and updated Interactions menu.
- Sheet presentation for Share, Trainer, and Ask AI dialogs on iPhone, with grabber handles.
- Navigation bar now smoothly fades when scrolling stories, with proper content inset restoration.
- Default theme changed from Light to Auto (follows system appearance).
- Dark mode now correctly overrides window interface style to match the selected NewsBlur theme.

Redesigned screens:
- Login screen redesigned with animated Metal shader waves and frosted glass card.
- Preferences moved from InAppSettingsKit to a new native PreferencesView.
- Premium upgrade view redesigned with Ask AI integration and Premium Pro tier.
- Ask AI and Share popovers redesigned with compact layouts and inline controls.
- Sync notification moved to a subtle top-right indicator in the navigation bar.

Mac Catalyst improvements:

- Auto-hide sidebar below 900pt window width.

- Adjusted popover menu positioning.
