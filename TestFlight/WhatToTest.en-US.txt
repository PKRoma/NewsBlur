NewsBlur 14 is a major update with new features, a redesigned interface, and full support for iOS/iPadOS 26 and macOS Tahoe.

New features:
- Dashboard: Shows stories from favorite feeds atop the feeds list, updated every five minutes. Add, remove, and rearrange feeds.
- Ask AI: Ask questions about any story using your choice of AI model, with a thinking mode toggle.
- Pinch-to-zoom images: Pinch any image to open a full-sized Quick Look preview.
- List and Magazine views: Two new story layouts. List shows compact rows; Magazine shows taller rows with larger thumbnails.
- Mark Story Read options: Mark read on scroll, selection, after an interval, or manually.
- Premium Pro tier with higher limits and additional features.
- Custom feed and folder icons.
- Collapse-all / expand-all button on All Site Stories.
- Unmute support for individual feeds.

iOS/iPadOS 26 and macOS Tahoe support:

- Sidebar toggle buttons for showing/hiding the feeds list.
- Transparent toolbar styling and updated Interactions menu.
- Sheet presentation for Share, Trainer, and Ask AI on iPhone.
- Nav bar fades smoothly when scrolling stories.
- Default theme changed from Light to Auto (follows system appearance).
